Notes for Week 1

Local Storage

  • You can set an item in local storage with the setItem()function.
  • You can retrieve an item in local storage with the getItem()function.

Master Moble UX

  • Design websites for the lowest moble modle first.
  • Put your buttons in a spot that is easy to reach by the user's thumbs.
  • Limit the amount of content the user has to think about or interact with.
  • Be creative with adds. Make your content the star of the show. AVOID POP UPS AND POP OVERS
